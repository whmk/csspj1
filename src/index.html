Simple Omelette Receipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Prepation time

  • Total:Approximately 10 minutes
  • Prepation:5minutes
  • Cooking:5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetable, cooked meats, herbs

Instuctions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed.You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for fluffier texture.
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omelette: Once the butter is melted and bubbing, pour in the eggs. Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.
  4. Add fillings(optional): When the eggs begin to set at edges but ate still slightly runny in the middle, sprinkle your chosen fillings over one half of the omelette.
  5. Flid and serve: As the omelette to cook, carefully lift one edge and flid it over the fillings.Let it cook for another minute, then slide it ont a plate.
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot, wiht additional salt and pepper if needed.
